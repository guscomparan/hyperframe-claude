Always use **green `#487d00`** as the karaoke caption accent (ACCENT in tiktok-studio
`videos/<name>/config.py`) — the block behind the active spoken word. Do NOT switch it per
video (declined a cyber-red option even on an alarm/danger-themed cybersecurity video).

**Why:** it's the proven house style with high legibility (white text on the green block).
**How to apply:** leave `ACCENT = "#487d00"` in every video's config; don't offer alternatives.
